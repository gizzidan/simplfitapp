FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does it cost?

Completely free for customers (you only have to pay your fitness professional). Providers pay a small service fee of 10% per transaction.

As a provider, what types of services can I provide through SimplFit?

Primarily, you have the ability to offer one-on-one sessions and group sessions. We also plan to give you the ability to sell e-books and host massive open online courses!

What type of services are offered?

Although we are still in development, we are recruiting all types of fitness professionals including: personal trainers, nutritionists, strength coaches, yoga instructors, sports-specific trainers, physical therapists, and many more.

Who should use SimplFit?

SimplFit is perfect for you if you don't know how to workout, have fitness goals that you are having trouble reaching, are too busy to find and consult with a great fitness professional, or would rather get healthier and learn about fitness from the comfort of your home.
